# Departure from Aurelia Station

Mira Voss stepped onto Aurelia Station with the Sun Key locked beneath her coat.
The Lantern Guild watched from the observation deck while Commander Ilya Ren sealed the airlock.

# The Old Rule

The Covenant of Ash declared that no pilot may activate the Sun Key beyond the orbit of Nereid.
Mira recorded the rule in the station archive before the vessel left for the Glass Sea.

# The Signal

At the Glass Sea, Mira met the Archivist and learned that the Sun Key opens a path to the lost city of Valen.
Commander Ilya Ren promised the Lantern Guild that Aurelia Station would remain protected.

# The Cartographer

Cartographer Sable mapped a quiet route from Valen to the moon of Nereid.
Mira Voss kept the Sun Key hidden while the Lantern Guild monitored every signal.

# The Broken Beacon

At dawn, the beacon above Aurelia Station repeated the Archivist's name.
Commander Ilya Ren ordered the Covenant of Ash to protect the station archive.

# The Glass Current

Mira Voss sailed through the Glass Sea with Cartographer Sable and the Sun Key.
The old rule still prohibited activating it outside Nereid orbit.

# The Hidden Archive

The Archivist revealed a sealed chamber beneath Valen containing maps of Aurelia Station.
The Lantern Guild guarded the chamber until Commander Ilya Ren arrived.

# Nereid's Promise

On Nereid, Mira Voss promised not to break the Covenant of Ash.
Cartographer Sable recorded the promise beside the Sun Key in the archive.

# The Last Signal

The beacon sent one final signal from Aurelia Station to the Glass Sea.
The Archivist told Mira Voss that Valen would remain safe.

# Homeward

Commander Ilya Ren welcomed Mira Voss back to Aurelia Station.
The Lantern Guild stored the Sun Key under the old rule of the Covenant of Ash.
